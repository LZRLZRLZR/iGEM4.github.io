Inspiration

Different from mammals such as human beings, the digestive system structure of birds is completely different from that of us. Routine oral administration methods, such as mice gavage, test the skill of the operator. At the same time, even skilled operators may have leakage and absorption errors during administration, resulting in random differences in dosage.

In addition, in the production and application stage, it is difficult for us to require every producer to have the expertly administration skills of the laboratory personnel, so it is very necessary to have a complete set of dosing instruments

mini-program functionality

Applets include front-end and back-end, as well as hardware (be incomplete).

The small program can automatically calculate the required drug volume by inputting the drug concentration, target dose and animal body in the front of the mobile phone. After the data is transferred to the background (cloud development background), the data is stored. Due to the lack of hardware personnel, we have stored data in the background as the information interaction between hardware and software.

When the phantom hardware receives the data stored in the background, it will determine the drug volume and give the drug according to the incoming data. So as to achieve the same precise administration as the dormitory boiling water machine and reduce the error.
