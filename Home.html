Antibiotic Resistance（ABR）-- A Mutual Challenge for Human

If not reversed, the annual toll caused by ABR will climb to 10,000,000 deaths in the next 30 years,and $ 100 trillion will be spent by 2050......

What's the cause?

Every year, the overuse of antibiotics in husbandary contributed to the soaring ABR as a main cause. The global consumption of antibiotics for animal feed was about 63,151 tons in 2010, China accounting for the most of the total(23%).

Yet，it’s not that simple.

Depite restrictive measures adopted by Chinese government,given the current situation of domestic productivity, lack of access to registered veterinary prescriptions, farmers in poverty-stricken rural areas rely on antibiotics to control animal disease and secure income.

Tetracycline Abuse in Animal Husbandry —— Resistance of Pathogen to Tetracycline was Enhanced

Tetracycline,as broad-spectrum antibiotic,are widely used as animal growth-promoting additives.However, heterologous expression of bacteriocins and toxins with potential toxicity to host bacteria restricts the efficient expression and industrious production.

Tet-resistant Salmonella

Abstract

According to statistics, about 60 billion broilers are now eaten on earth each year, and trillions of eggs are consumed each year. American CDC estimates Salmonella cause about 1.35 million infections, 26,500 hospitalizations, and 420 deaths in the United States every year. Food is the source for most of these illnesses. In order to reduce the pollution caused by salmonella disease, our team has developed Prickly Chicky to kill chicken with tetracycline resistance of salmonella in the body.

In our system, E.coli delivers plasmids to salmonella through conjugations. T7RNA polymerase was generated by the expression of the ‘AND’ logic structure of tetracycline resistant transcription factor tTA and salmonella specific transcription factor SsrB. T7RNA polymerase binds to the T7 promoter to further express orphan toxin in large quantities to kill salmonella. To improve OrtT retention ratio in plasmid, Nostoc punctiforme (Npu) DnaE was expressed together with OrtT as intein. The intein can be removed automatically during processing.
