MODEL

Overview

The part of cell A in the internal model of University of Leuven is mainly to simulate the concentration of leucine and AHL in and out of cells.

Cell A uses E.coli cells that knock out tar and tsr, so that they are not sensitive to leucine. The recombinant plasmid is introduced into cell A, which contains three expression systems:

  • cl repressor expression system
  • AHL enzyme expression system
  • transaminase B expression system and leucine production system

pic1.Cell A System

The specific expression process mainly include transcription, translation, complexation and oligomerization kinetics, protein production kinetics, degradation and diffusion.

Following Systems

1) cl repressor protein expression system

The repressor characteristics of cl protein are related to temperature, when it is higher than a specific temperature the repressor can’t combine to DNAs thus there’s no repression occurring. The system uses constitutive promoters, so the transcription rate is only related to the type of promoters. After transcripting into cl-mRNA, it starts to translate into cl protein, which needs to be polymerized to form [Cl]2 to act as the repressor.

2) AHL enzyme expression system

The system uses λ promoter to control transcription, which is repressed by [Cl]2 when the temperature is lower than the specific temperature, so Hill function is used to simulate the transcription rate before and after inhibition. LuxI controlled by the promoter can catalyze the production of AHL after transcription and translation, and AHL can permeate out of the cells to play a role. After transcription and translation, LuxR forms heterodimer with AHL,and then complexes to form tetramer [LuxR/AHL]2, which can be used to activate the transaminase B expression system.

3) Transaminase B expression system and leucine production system:

This system uses LuxR promoter to start transcription, and after the transcription and translation of ilvE, transaminase B can further catalyze various biochemical reactions of glutamic and other substances in the system to produce leucine, and leucine penetrates into the extracellular system to play a role. The products of Ag43-YFP transcription and translation are mainly related to cell aggregation and it can make the cells show yellow fluorescence.

Because of the assumption that the substrate pool can not be depleted and the concentration (or amount of molecules) of substrate in the cell is constant，we assume the content of glutamate is 100000, and the content of aKIC and aKG is 1000 in the model.

There are Cl and [Cl]2 in the initial system, otherwise, part of the calculation will be meaningless. In the modeling, the cl content is 500, [Cl]2 content is 615000.

For the same reason, the initial time can’t be zero, so we use 1×10-20 instead.
